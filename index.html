Anna: A Recipe ®

Sugar, spice, and everything nice?! Not quite. Below are the ingredients needed to make up Anna.

Ingredients:

  • 2 Heaping Cups of Sarcasm
  • A 12 Hour Medical Drama Binge
  • An Hour of Cuddles a Day with Kitty Cat Garth
  • Enough True Crime to Keep You Vigilant, but not Scared
  • Lots of Laughter
Now let's learn about each ingredient and the importance it has towards our recipe.

Sarcasm

I am not explaining sarcasm to you. You have it or you don't.

Medical Dramas

Dr. McDreamy, Dr.McSteamy, and HUGH LAURIE as Dr. House! YES PLEASE! Stand around and season the pot by yelling loads of medical terminology that you barely understand into it.

Kitty Cat Garth

A long day at work is quickly washed away with some kitty cat cuddles from my best bud Garth. He was named after Garth Alagar from Wayne's World NOT Brooks.

True Crime

This is a careful measurement. I would not recommend binge watching Forensic Files more than 5 hours at a time. Doing so will make you suspicious of everyone in your life.

Laughter

Laugh directly into the pan you are cooking in because I keep forgetting this is a "recipe". Laughter is achieved by watching funny shows and movies or hanging out with your funny friends.
